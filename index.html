Most anticipated games of 2014

With the spring and summer conference seasons in full swing (PAX East, GDC and E3), it's time to start getting hyped about this year's most most anticipated games.

Hyper Light Drifter

Hyper Light Drifter is an upcoming 2D action role-playing game developed by Heart Machine, due to be released in late 2014. The game is a homage to 8-bit and 16-bit games of the late 20th century, and is considered a combination of The Legend of Zelda: A Link to the Past and Diablo.

Transistor

Transistor is a forthcoming science fiction action RPG video game by Supergiant Games. It is planned for release on May 20, 2014 on Microsoft Windows and PlayStation 4 platforms, with other platforms possible in the future.

Crawl

Crawl is a competitive multiplayer dungeon crawler where your friends control the monsters. Kill the hero to instantly swap places, and it's your turn to crawl! It's a race to gain enough experience and loot to take on the dungeon's boss, cooperatively controlled by the other players!

ROCKETSROCKETSROCKETS

ROCKETS fire ROCKETS at ROCKETS in this action-arcade arena-combat festival-of-fireworks! If multiplayer and single-player fast-paced combat not your cup of tea, try our figure-skating-esque ZEN MODE where ROCKETS learn to LOVE.

Below

Below is a narrative dungeon-delving RPG card game you can play in your web browser. It combines the old-school delights of exploration, looting treasure and subterranean peril with a contemporary approach to storytelling.

Quadrilateral Cowboy

Hacking. Dial tone. Cyberpunk. Cassette decks. Robotics. Espionage. Sabotage. Suitcase decks. Simulators. Brainbox injectors. When you have a top-of-the-line hacking deck armed with a 56.6k modem and a staggering 256k RAM, it means just one thing: you answer only to the highest bidder.
